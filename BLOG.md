# Streamline Your Product Adoption with the New Appcues Node for n8n

We're excited to announce that Velocity BPA has just released a powerful new community node for n8n: the Appcues integration!

## The Challenge of Product Adoption Data

If you're using Appcues to drive user onboarding and engagement, you know how valuable that behavioral data is. But getting Appcues to talk to your other tools—your CRM, data warehouse, support systems, or analytics platforms—often means wrestling with APIs or paying for expensive integration platforms.

That's where automation comes in.

## Introducing n8n-nodes-appcues

Our new Appcues node brings the full power of Appcues directly into your n8n workflows. Now you can seamlessly connect your product adoption platform with hundreds of other services, all without writing a single line of code.

## What Can You Do With It?

The n8n-nodes-appcues node gives you comprehensive access to:

- **Users**: Sync user data bidirectionally, update profiles, and trigger actions based on user behavior
- **Flows**: Monitor and manage your onboarding flows programmatically
- **Segments**: Access and utilize your user segments for targeted campaigns
- **Checklists**: Track checklist completion and integrate that data into your workflows
- **NPS Surveys**: Pull survey responses and route them to your analytics or support tools
- **Analytics**: Extract valuable insights and push them wherever you need them

## Real-World Use Cases

Imagine automatically:
- Adding users who complete specific flows to targeted email campaigns
- Triggering support outreach when NPS scores fall below a threshold
- Syncing Appcues segments to your CRM for better sales intelligence
- Logging all user interactions to your data warehouse for comprehensive analytics
- Creating Slack alerts when key checklists are completed

## Getting Started

Installation is simple. Just run:


npm install n8n-nodes-appcues


Then restart your n8n instance, and you'll find the Appcues node ready to use in your workflow editor.

For detailed documentation and examples, check out our GitHub repository: https://github.com/Velocity-BPA/n8n-nodes-appcues
